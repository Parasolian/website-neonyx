Our Vision

The Neonyx Philosophy

A synthesis of raw organic geological texture and modern lighting physics.

Onyx: The Base

We believe great interior design begins with raw, high-contrast structural elegance. Using dark marble, textured slate, rich walnuts, and obsidian stones, we lay down a sophisticated backdrop that feels grounded and premium.

Neon: The Glow

Light is the paint of the modern era. We use sophisticated cove lighting, architectural LEDs, glowing brass fixtures, and soft ambient illumination to highlight textures, extend space, and create dynamic atmospheres at night.

Harmony: The Living

We tailor every detail to the occupant. A room should not just look like a photograph; it must behave like a sanctuary. By balancing shadow and radiance, we deliver comfortable, functional, and awe-inspiring living spaces.

How We Work

Our Design Services

From conceptual sketches to full turn-key handovers, we construct beautiful living environments.

1. Design & Space Planning

We craft spatial layouts that optimize space, light, and movement. Including 3D rendering walkthroughs, color scheme creation, and materials sourcing.

2. Custom Lighting Schemes

Light changes everything. We curate custom lighting schemes involving indirect LED installation, architectural spotlight design, and fine sculptural light selections.

3. Furniture & Decor Styling

We source bespoke furniture, custom cabinetry, luxury carpets, window treatments, and artwork to style your home with Neonyx's tailored fingerprint.

4. Project Supervision

We supervise contractors, lighting engineers, and decorators to execute the design blueprints exactly as drawn, delivering stress-free handovers.
